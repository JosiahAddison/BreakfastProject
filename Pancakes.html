Pancakes

Pancakes are likely the most popular breakfast to make. It's commonly made with flour, eggs, milk, and pancake mix, and can be customized and accessorized to however the maker sees fit.

An average-sized pancake contains 93 calories, 2 grams of protein, 15 grams of carbohydrate, 0 grams of fiber and 2 grams of sugar.
